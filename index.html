Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the carbon footprint calculations?
Our proprietary algorithm combines product lifecycle assessment data with local ANZ supply chain factors for region-specific accuracy. We partner with certified environmental data providers and update our database monthly.
Which retailers are supported?
We currently support major retailers including Woolworths, Countdown, Coles, and hundreds of local stores. We're continuously expanding our retailer network and database.
Can I use this for online purchases?
Yes! You can scan digital receipts from online purchases or manually search our product database. We support both in-store and online shopping tracking.
Is my shopping data private?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never sell your data. Your shopping habits remain completely private and are only used to provide you with personalized recommendations.
